About MyWave

A youth movement born from the ocean

Why we exist

MyWave promotes life balance through surf and water sports. We want to help children, teenagers and families discover the strength and joy of doing water sports in nature. The power of the ocean can help you become stronger, happier and more balanced — a healthy alternative to endless hours online in front of devices.

Who started MyWave

Hi! I am Arthur, 14 years old, and surfing is my passion. I grew up just like many other kids — surrounded by screens, noise, and endless scrolling. But the first time I tried surfing, everything changed. The ocean became a place where I could feel at my best. Surfing taught me patience, courage, how to trust myself and brought lots of new people and friends in my life. Though intense training I reached a competitive level in surf and now I travel and compete at international level and see parts of the world I never imagined I’d visit. Being out in the water beats scrolling any day. Nature has given me so much more than any screen ever could — real joy, real adventure, real memories. I want to share that feeling with other kids and families. I want them to know that out there, beyond the phones and tablets, there is a whole world waiting. A world where you can feel the wind, hear the waves, and discover your own strength. With support from family, friends and surf community I want to launch MyWave to help you discover the beauty of surfing and other water sports. Surfing changed my life, maybe it can change yours too!

What we believe

MyWave is here to help you discover aware outdoor water sports and encourage youth to live more offline and more alive. We believe the sea, the ocean is one of the best places to build confidence, friendships and real-life skills — far away from constant screens and notifications.

Our programs

More surf time, less screen time

For schools, teens and families who want to connect with the sea, be active and discover the magic of water sports.

For schools

MyWave Sport Day

A full school day by the sea: classes try surf or other water sports with certified instructors, while phones stay in bags.

For schools

Ocean Week

A week that mixes school projects, ocean sports and talks about digital balance, mental health and life skills learned from the sea.

For teens

Summer Surf Camps

Fun and immersive camps where teens surf, sail, SUP and build friendships away from screens, with coaches and ocean time every day.

For families

Family Summer Camps

Parents and kids enjoy shared surf and water sport sessions, reconnecting with each other and nature in a relaxed ocean setting.
